## ğŸ“¸ Image Specifications

### Profile Photo (`/images/profile.jpg`)
- **Recommended size**: 400x400px or 500x500px
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Style**: Professional headshot with clean background
- **Used in**: Hero section

### Company Logos (`/images/logos/`)
- **Recommended size**: 200x80px (width x height)
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background preferred
- **Style**: Official company logos
- **Files needed**:
  - `lexmark.png` - Lexmark International
  - `tcs.png` - Tata Consultancy Services
  - `uw.png` - University of Washington

### Project Images (`/images/projects/`)
- **Recommended size**: 800x600px or 1200x900px (4:3 ratio)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Style**: Screenshots or hero images of your projects
- **Files needed**:
  - `printiq.jpg` - PrintIQ Studio interface/screenshot
  - `cloudsuite.jpg` - CloudSuite Financials dashboard/screenshot
  - `financetracker.jpg` - Personal Finance Tracker interface
  - `taskminder.jpg` - TaskMinder Pro interface
